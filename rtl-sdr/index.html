RTL-SDR⌗

This weekend I finally recieved my RTL-SDR USB dongle in the mail. It was recommended to me by a coworker as a great way to break into SDR, without having to pay for something like the HackRF or BladeRF.

What is RTL-SDR?⌗

rtl-sdr.com explains the following:

RTL-SDR is a very cheap ~$25 USB dongle that can be used as a computer based radio scanner for receiving live radio signals in your area (no internet required). Depending on the particular model it could receive frequencies from 500 kHz up to 1.75 GHz.

The price tag makes it a great option for people getting started in SDR!!
